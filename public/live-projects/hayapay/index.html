About HayaPay

Leading payroll outsourcing partner in Sub Saharan Africa, powered by world class technology from PaySpace and Workday.

Our Story

HayaPay is Africa's leading HR and payroll solution provider, dedicated to revolutionizing how businesses manage their most valuable asset — their people.

As the cloud native leader powered by PaySpace and Workday, we combine cutting edge technology with deep local expertise to deliver payroll solutions that transform business operations across Sub-Saharan Africa.

With offices in the UK, USA, and Ghana, we serve businesses of all sizes across 43+ countries, ensuring compliance, efficiency, and cost effectiveness in every payroll we process.
